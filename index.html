The Hog Rider is a Rare card that is unlocked from the Builder's Workshop (Arena 5). It spawns a fast, single-target, building-targeting, melee, ground troop with high hitpoints and high damage. He is able to jump over the river. He appears as a dark-skinned shirtless man with blue eyes, dark brown eyebrows, a beard, a mohawk, a golden body piercing in his left ear, wears two golden wristbands, and a brown loincloth with team-colored belt. He wields a hammer tied with a team-colored ribbon, and is riding a hog. A Hog Rider card costs 4 Elixir to deploy.
